#nignx-rewrite
https://nginx.org/en/docs/http/ngx_http_rewrite_module.html#rewrite

```nginx
Syntax:	rewrite regex replacement [flag];
Default:	—
Context:	server, location, if
```

- flag
	- last
		- 继续匹配
	- break
		- 停止匹配
	- redirect
		- 返回重定向
		- 302
	- permanet
		- 301
